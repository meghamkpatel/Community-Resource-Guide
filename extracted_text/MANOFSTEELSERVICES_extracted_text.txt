About Us
My Mission
I offer a service for the youth, specifically the males, to teach essential life tools, to mentor and encourage them. Through the business, I plan to positively influence and add value to the lives of the individuals who participate by developing positive attitudes and character while teaching them to take full advantage of their opportunities.
