of all children.
The Latino Educational Achievement Partnership (LEAP) empowers children in Durham to achieve academic success by providing a high-quality, dual-language preschool and on-going support throughout middle school.
​
LEAP considers all families who participate in our programs as part of the LEAP Community. We make sure that our families get connected with the Durham’s resources based on their individual needs.
