Sister Cities of Durham
The City of Durham has nine sister cities around the globe:
Arusha, Tanzania
Durham, United Kingdom
Kostroma, Russia
*
(suspended August 15, 2022)
Toyama, Japan
Zhuzhou, Hunan Province, China
Celaya, Mexico
Kavala, Greece
Sibiu, Romania
Tilarán, Costa Rica
*Note: On August 15, 2022, Durham City Council voted to suspend relations with Kostroma, Russia, based on concerns about Russia's invasion of Ukraine. For more information, see the
statement (PDF)
and
resolution (PDF)
adopted by the Sister Cities Board of Directors.
The city also has two friendship cities:
Kunshan, China
Xianning, China
Activities and Events
Activities and events for our sister cities are handled through the non-profit organization, Sister Cities of Durham, Inc.
Sister Cities of Durham promotes international friendship, intercultural understanding, and economic development with exchange programs in the areas of Education, the Arts, Medicine, Local Government, Business, and Environment.
The major program is the Annual Festival of Nations, featuring Durham's 9 Sister Cities and 18 other countries connected to Durham.
