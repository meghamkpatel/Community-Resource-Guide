Family Pool Calendar
For your whole family…
Swim
The aquatics facilities at Hollow Rock Racquet & Swim Club consist of a family pool and splash pad, as well as a year-round fitness and lap pool. Our family pool features a diving board and a waterslide. Our 25-meter, eight lane fitness and lap pool is heated and allows for swimming in the winter months.
Learn More
Racquet
Hollow Rock Racquet & Swim Club is an award-winning 22 tennis court facility with some of the best maintained courts in North Carolina. We have 19 clay courts and three hard courts, of which 18 are lit including the three hard courts. All of our courts are exceptionally maintained and available year-round, weather permitting.  We also have six permanently lined pickleball courts!
Learn More
Events
Enjoy events with your family throughout the year. We take pride in the quality of our social events at the club. They are well-attended and fun—a great way to meet a variety of members! Most events require you to sign up ahead of time, so look to the calendar for exact dates and get in touch if you want to participate!

Courts & Pools
Hollow Rock Racquet & Swim Club is one of North Carolina's premiere, member-owned clubs. We have been a staple in the Triangle sports community for over 40 years, and promote a friendly, welcoming and fun environment for all of our members and guests.
