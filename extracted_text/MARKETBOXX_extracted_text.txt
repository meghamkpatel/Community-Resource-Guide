What is MarketBoxx?
MarketBoxx is a nonprofit organization created to combat food insecurity on college campuses. As alumni of North Carolina A&T State University, the founders of MarketBoxx understand how a lack of food resources can affect college students. We also see the direct impact of food deserts that surround many college campuses and alter the health and nutrition of college students.
Experiencing food insecurity as former college students is something we understand and will fight to combat.
