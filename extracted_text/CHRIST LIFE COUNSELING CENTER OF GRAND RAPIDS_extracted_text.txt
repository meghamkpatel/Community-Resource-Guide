Christian Counseling Center is a ministry of Pine Rest Christian Mental Health Care Services.
The Christian Counseling Center provides the communities of northeast Grand Rapids and northern Kent County with quality in-person and virtual behavioral health services by licensed clinicians, including therapy and psychiatric services for children, adolescents, adults, and families.
