church workday
We are planning to have a spring work day on Saturday, May 11th at 8am. We will be cleaning up our yard and adding mulch in the flower beds. Bring yard equipment if you have it! Pleas sign up in the foyer if you can help.
secret sister brunch
Please check the table in the foyer to pick up any gifts/cards for you! Also, our Secret Sister Brunch reveal is on Saturday, May 18th at 10am at the church. The main dish will be provided, but we ask that you bring a brunch side dish to share (fruit, quiche, pastries, etc.). Feel free to bring a last gift for your Secret Sister too!
tagless tag sale
Mark your calendars for our Tagless Tag Sale on Saturday, May 25th! All proceeds will benefit Brook of Hope Orphanage in Kenya. Donations of goods will be accepted the week of May 20th. Reach out to Kellene Limpert for more info at 860-514-3529.
MOMS TOGETHER GROUP
Moms Together meets the first Thursday of the month at 9:30am to 11am in the fellowship hall! This group provides an opportunity to meet and connect with stay-at-home moms in your community. If you feel isolated or lonely, need a little encouragement or would just like to have a cup of coffee with other moms, please join us! Open to young moms, grandmoms, auntie moms and all women caring for young children. Refreshments and childcare provided. Please bring a friend and don’t forget to sign up in the foyer. kid’s kloset
The Kid’s Kloset is once again open! Kid’s Kloset provides clothing for children who are in foster care. Infant through teen sized clothing is needed. There is a donation bin in the fellowship hall by the back door. If you have any questions, please contact Nanette Williams at 860-857-3531.
Grace kids wednesday nights
Our Wednesday night program, Grace Kids, meets every Wednesday evening at 6pm! This is for kids pre-k through 5th grade and is a fun night of learning God’s Word, games and good old fashioned fellowship!
Hard Pressed Youth Group
Our Wednesday night youth meeting (Hard Pressed) meets Wednesday evenings at 6pm! This is for grades 6th-12th and is a fun group of teens that meet every week. Contact Pastor Chad for more info!
SUNDAY SCHOOL
Sunday school for all ages from 8:45am to 9:45am. Please visit the foyer for class and teacher information. We will have classes from Pre-K through adults. Plan to attend Sunday school hour!
Contact Elaine Carlson at 860-599-4266 with any questions or for more information on small groups!
