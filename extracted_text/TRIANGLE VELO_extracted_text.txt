About Triangle Velo
Triangle Velo is an elite amateur cycling team based in the Research Triangle of North Carolina.  We use cycling and our presence in the community to raise awareness of our sponsors and charitable organizations we promote.  Triangle Velo is a 501(c)3 nonprofit organization, and all proceeds generated from our events are used for our mission:
● Increase awareness and help raise funds for the fight against childhood obesity
● Provide active, healthy activities for people in the Triangle area
● Advocate for youth cycling and cycling related initiatives
