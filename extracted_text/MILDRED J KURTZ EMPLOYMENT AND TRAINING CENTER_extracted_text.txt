About The Center
The Mildred J. Kurtz Employment and Training Center is committed to ending joblessness and alleviating poverty in the Hayti District of Durham, to bringing hope and job opportunities to the most vulnerable and challenged people in the area, and to providing basic job-related training and assistance needed to procure employment.
Learn more
How it Began
The Mildred J. Kurtz Employment and Training Center was founded in 2017 by Pastor William V. Lucas of the First Chronicles Church to honor his grandmother Mildred J. Kurtz.
