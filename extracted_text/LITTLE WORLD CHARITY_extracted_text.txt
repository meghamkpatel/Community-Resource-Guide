Our Mission
is to brighten the lives of children in hospitals, special needs homes, and libraries.  We create and deploy unique, whimsical, animated displays for kids of all ages to activate with the press of a button.  We charge no fees, are 100% volunteer and 0% overhead. We've grown to 22 locations and create 300,000 smiles a year. Won't you help us?
Basic Beliefs
What are the most important gifts one can give?
Joy, inspiration.
Who needs it?
Children (of all ages), those dealing with illness, and our entire community.
Where can we touch the most hearts?
At our hospitals, libraries, special needs , and other public venues.
