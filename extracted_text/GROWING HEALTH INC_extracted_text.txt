Frequently Asked Questions
What is Grow Therapy?
Grow Therapy connects clients, therapists, and insurance companies to make mental healthcare simple to access.
Who is the right therapist for me?
We make it easy to find a therapist who looks like you and can support your needs. The right therapist is one who ensures you feel safe and comfortable. If you need help choosing, read
these tips
or contact our matching team via email at support@growtherapy.com or by phone at 786-244-7711.
How do you vet your therapists?
We conduct an intensive interview process to ensure our therapists have the skills, training, and experience to help you grow.
How much will it cost?
People who use their insurance save an average of 73% on the cost of therapy. Sessions cost an average of $24 with insurance, but will vary depending on your plan. Contact your insurance company for more details.
