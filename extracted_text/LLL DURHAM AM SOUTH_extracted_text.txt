Meetings are informal and are open to pregnant women, mothers, babies and children. If you have questions about breastfeeding or just would like to meet other breastfeeding mothers, this is the meeting for you.
La Leche League Leaders are experienced mothers who have breastfed their own babies and who have been trained and accredited by La Leche League International to help mothers and mothers-to-be with all aspects of breastfeeding. They are available by phone whenever you have breastfeeding questions or concerns.

La Leche League of NC
La Leche League Leaders are volunteer breastfeeding counselors who have breastfed their own babies and are active in providing breastfeeding support in their communities. Leaders are trained and accredited by La Leche League International to help families with all aspects of breastfeeding. They are available by phone, email, text, or social media whenever you have questions or concerns.
