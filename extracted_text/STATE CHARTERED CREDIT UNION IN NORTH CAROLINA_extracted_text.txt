2023 North Carolina Credit Union Division Annual Report
The
2023 Annual Report
of the North Carolina Credit Union Division provides financial information for North Carolina state-chartered credit unions, along with information regarding the Credit Union Division and the Credit Union Commission.  As of December 31, 2023, there were 29 natural person state-chartered credit unions in North Carolina serving approximately 3.2 million members.  The Credit Union Division also supervises a state-chartered corporate credit union.
