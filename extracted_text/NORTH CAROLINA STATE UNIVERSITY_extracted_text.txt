Astounding New Alums
Our brand-new alumni hail from 42 states and 63 countries. They range from 18 years old to 67 years old. And 779 of them are the first in their family to earn a college degree.
By every metric, this year’s graduating class excelled and exceeded expectations. We can’t wait to see what’s next.
