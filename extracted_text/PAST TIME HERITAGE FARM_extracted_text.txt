Past Time Heritage Farm, Inc.
is a privately-owned, 501(c)3, non-profit, educational and historical corporation which sponsors or supports projects and activities that preserve and display for private or public viewing, buildings, artifacts, practices and traditions of small tobacco farms in the region. The corporation is directed by a 7 member Board of Directors and an Advisory Board since 2001. We are funded by annual fundraisers, private and public donations.

Past Time Heritage Farm, Inc.
exists to preserve the legacy of small tobacco farming as practiced in Chatham County, N.C. and surrounding areas from the 1940s to the Post World War II 1970s. It also seeks to promote -- especially among farm family descendants -- an awareness and appreciation of the life-styles and family traditions of the men and women who shaped and were shaped by the occupation of farming in that period.
