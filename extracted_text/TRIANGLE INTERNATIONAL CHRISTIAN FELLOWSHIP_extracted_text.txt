ABOUT OUR CHURCH
Triangle International Church is a Bible believing, Independent, Pentecostal Church. Our church is committed to proclaiming the truth of Jesus Christ through His Word - the Bible. The truth of Scripture is relevant for today’s world, and we are living in a day when people face challenges and sometimes seemingly insurmountable difficulties. We believe that God’s Word has the answer to every circumstance that we encounter. Here at Triangle International Christian Fellowship, we strive to present the principles contained in Scripture so we may better understand God‘s character, His purposes, and His will for our lives with the help of the Holy Spirit. We are located Triangle area, North Carolina.
