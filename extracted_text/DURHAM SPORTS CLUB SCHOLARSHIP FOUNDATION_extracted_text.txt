ABOUT US
Welcome to the Durham Sports Club! We are a group of men and women from Durham and the surrounding area who share a mutual interest in sports. We usually meet twice a month for lunch, fellowship, and a speaker from the wonderful world of sports. Our speakers range from world-renowned sports personalities to professional athletes, coaches, referees, authors, and commentators.
We have special events throughout the year, including a “Night Out With the Durham Bulls”, the “Durham High School Scholar-Athlete Awards Night” and more. Within the pages of this website, you’ll find plenty of information about the Durham Sports Club and all our events. Thanks for visiting!
