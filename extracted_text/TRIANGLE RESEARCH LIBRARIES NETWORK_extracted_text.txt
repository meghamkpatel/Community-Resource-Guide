Triangle Research Libraries Network (TRLN) is a collaborative organization of
Duke University
,
North Carolina Central University
,
North Carolina State University
, and
The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill
, the purpose of which is to marshal the financial, human, and information resources of their research libraries through cooperative efforts in order to create a rich and unparalleled knowledge environment that furthers the universities’ teaching, research, learning, and service missions.
