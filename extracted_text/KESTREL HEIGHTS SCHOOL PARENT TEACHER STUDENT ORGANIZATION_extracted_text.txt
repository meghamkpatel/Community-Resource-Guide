OUR MISSION
We are a small, diverse, and inclusive learning community in Durham that empowers our Scholars to sharpen academic knowledge, demonstrate creative expression and expand leadership abilities to prepare for success in high school, college, and beyond.
OUR VISION
Believing that all children can succeed in a safe and challenging environment, Kestrel Heights Charter School (KHCS) nurtures a community of lifelong learners. KHCS fosters this community by setting high expectations, modeling characteristics of responsible citizens, and focusing on the development of the whole person.
