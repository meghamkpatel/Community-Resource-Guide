We are on the move for God with a vision! We are well aware of what God has called us to do; and we are committed to carrying out holistic ministry. We are a multicultural Christian community that consist of two congregations: the predominately African American Nehemiah Church and the
predominately Latino Nehemías Espíritu de Vida.
