REENTRY SUCCESS
Wounded Healers of North Carolina is an emerging Reentry Community Organization led by those with the lived experience of incarceration. With perseverance and support, Wounded Healers have successfully transitioned back to their community and now resolve to use their own transformation experience, bolstered by certifications as peer support specialists, to help others achieve the same.
