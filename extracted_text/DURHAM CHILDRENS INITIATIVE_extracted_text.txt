Who We Are
DCI builds pathways to equity. We envision a future in which every youth graduates from high school ready for college or career.
OUR WORK
DCI is the most comprehensive cradle to college and career pipeline of
high-quality services for youth and their families
in the state of North Carolina. We do this through our values of:
Respect
In all that we do,
treat everyone as we would want to be treated
