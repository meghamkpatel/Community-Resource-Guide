Recognizing the need for a hybrid writing support group, we formed our charitable organization to raise awareness about mental health challenges. As an up-and-coming writing workshop, we are eager to help beginning authors write short stories, novels, and/or books of poetry. We also serve children through our  Write-to-Read Program.  It is hoped that some of our authors will use their writings to experience catharsis--healing through writing.  We eagerly accept the challenge of becoming a contributing member to the mental health community.

Lagniappe Writers Workshop  is a hybrid writing support group providing safe spaces for new authors to receive instruction and feedback throughout the writing process. Additionally, we serve within our community by demonstrating to young people how they can become better readers through writing. Furthermore, we provide access to authors and illustrators as well as publishing guidance.  LWW is a charitable organization dedicated to donating a portion of its proceeds from the sale of literature to support the mental health community.
