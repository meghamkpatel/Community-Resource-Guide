Welcome!
We are so glad you're here.
United Campus Christian Fellowship (UCCF) is a multi campus,  multi denominational campus ministry. We engage with students, faculty and staff in the greater area of Grand Rapids MI, specifically from the campuses of Grand Valley State University, Grand Rapids Community College, and Kendall College of Art and Design. Check out our opportunities to get involved in Open Table, Bible studies/small groups,  and unique service opportunities!
