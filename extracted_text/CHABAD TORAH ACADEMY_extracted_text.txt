Welcome to Chabad Torah Academy
Western Wake County's Premier Jewish Day School
Preschool- Grade 4
​
Chabad Torah Academy strives to provide academic excellence and positive character development through our merged curriculum of Judaic and General Studies. Each aspect of the curriculum supports the other so the children see a seamless presence of meaning, purpose and holiness in all that they learn. Our Chabad educational model is one that instills a love of Hashem, a love of Torah and a love for every fellow Jew.
