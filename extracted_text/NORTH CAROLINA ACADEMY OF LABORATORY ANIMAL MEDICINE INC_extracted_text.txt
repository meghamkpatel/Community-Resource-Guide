Welcome to NCALAM
The North Carolina Academy of Laboratory Animal Medicine, Inc. promotes communication and education for individuals interested in furthering their knowledge or improvement of laboratory animal medicine in North Carolina. This is accomplished by providing a forum for the exchange of ideas and high quality continuing education programs for our members.

The North Carolina Academy of Laboratory Animal Medicine (NCALAM) is a veterinary non-profit organization dedicated to providing educational opportunities and a network for all North Carolina veterinarians interested in laboratory animal medicine (LAM).
