Triangle Privacy Research Hub
Practical Privacy Solutions
Promoting the Innovative and Ethical Use of Data
The Triangle Privacy Research Hub (TPRH) is a non-profit organization that brings together academics, industry leaders, students, and policy advocates in the North Carolina Research Triangle to address some of society’s most pressing issues through the innovative and ethical use of data. TPRH promotes the research of privacy-protecting policy solutions to advance the use of data across five areas:
Access to Information
Gender and Sexual Violence
Education
Healthcare
National Security/Law Enforcement
Learn about our work within each area
Connecting the North Carolina Research Triangle on Data and Privacy
TPRH strives to facilitate collaboration between different universities in the North Carolina Research Triangle region by connecting academics, industry leaders, professionals, students, and policy advocates
