District 3 Meeting
Mark your calendar for the upcoming District 3 meeting where attendees will discuss important district issues and regional educational matters.
August 11 – 14, 2024
Mobile, AL
Contact
District 3 Contacts
At the district meeting, NABP and
AACP
have a unique opportunity to address not only professional issues affecting today’s pharmacy practice, but also educational matters influencing tomorrow’s pharmacists. Board members and faculty come together to discuss regional and national issues during the meeting. In preparation for the 121
st
NABP Annual Meeting, candidates seeking nomination for 2025-2026 Executive Committee member positions will be announced and voted upon, and resolutions for consideration will also be presented.
Attendees may suggest resolutions for consideration at the district meetings. Resolutions approved by the districts will be submitted for consideration at the NABP 121
st
Annual Meeting. To submit a resolution request,
please fill out this form
and send it to
ExecOffice@nabp.pharmacy
.
