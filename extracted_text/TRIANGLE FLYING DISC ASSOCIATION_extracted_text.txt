Who We Are
Since 1990, Triangle Ultimate has existed to build, grow, and sustain a happy, healthy, and thriving community for people in the Triangle region of North Carolina through the sport of Ultimate Frisbee. We achieved
non-profit status
in 1998.
What We Do
Triangle Ultimate organizes adult and youth Ultimate Frisbee leagues, tournaments, camps, and clinics.
Our mission is to
collaboratively build a community emphasizing accessibility, relationships, and engagement through learning, playing, and teaching the sport of Ultimate.
