International Services Assistance Fund (ISAF)
Foundations, Institutes, + Science Associations
« Back to RTP directory
The mission of International Services Assistance Fund (ISAF) is to encourage women’s reproductive health, rights and empowerment. ISAF works to fulfill this mission through research and education, particularly by facilitating the use of safe, inexpensive and non-coercive family planning methods, specifically the quinacrine pellet method of nonsurgical permanent female contraception.
