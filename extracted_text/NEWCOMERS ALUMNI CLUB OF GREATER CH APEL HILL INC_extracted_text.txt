oin Us for Meet and Greet at Carolina 1663 Restaurant -- and Stay for Dinner!
All active and prospective members are welcome to share a drink and a snack and make some new friends at Newcomers monthly Meet and Greet. You will enjoy special drink pricing and a 20% discount on dinner.
(See separate event on calendar to make dinner reservations.)
6:00 pm
:  A brief introduction to the
Carrboro ArtsCenter
by Heather Gerni, Gallery Coordinator.   She will explain all that the ArtsCenter has to offer.    There will also be a November Newcomers Public Art Show, hosted for the full month of November by the ArtsCenter.  Participant artists will be Newcomers and Alumni Newcomers.  The Reception will be the Second Friday Artwalk on
Friday, November 14 from 6 pm to 9 pm
.  All are welcome.
6:30 pm
:
RAFFLE!
Prizes offered by the Carrboro ArtsCenter are a Membership to the ArtsCenter and also tickets to a theater show.  There will be a future group discount on a theater show to be determined.
Meet and Greet is a great way to meet new friends and share recommendations about restaurants, cultural events, medical providers, and contractors.
The Newcomers Website can be found at
www.newcomersofchapelhill.org
Meet and Greet is usually the first Monday of every month, so mark your calendars and come whenever you can.
Please note:  if the first Monday of the month falls on a holiday, Meet and Greet may be rescheduled. Be sure to check the Event Calendar.
