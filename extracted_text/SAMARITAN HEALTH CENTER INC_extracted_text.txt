Welcome to Samaritan Health Center
A free and charitable clinic in Durham, North Carolina.
At Samaritan Health Center, we envision a community where all people have what they need to flourish.
We provide accessible, holistic healthcare that focuses on physical needs, as well as the social, emotional, and spiritual issues that affect health, regardless of ability to pay. We look forward to meeting you.
Services
Whether you need a check-up, treatment for a minor ailment, or ongoing care, we are here to help.
Our services are focused on delivering compassionate care and improving the health and well-being of all individuals.
