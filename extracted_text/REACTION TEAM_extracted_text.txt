OUR PARTNERSHIP
We are proud to partner with organizations to provide comprehensive support to those in need. During the COVID-19 pandemic, we have provided assistance to stranded students and laborers, distributed medical kits and food, and helped individuals reunite with their loved ones for a final goodbye.
In addition to providing immediate assistance, our organization also aims to raise awareness about the importance of protecting yourself and your family while abroad. We are happy to provide resources and guidance on legal issues, abuse prevention, and other important topics. Our team is made up of active volunteers, including advisors and team members.
