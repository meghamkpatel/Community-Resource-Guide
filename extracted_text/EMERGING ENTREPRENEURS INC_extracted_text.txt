Welcome to Emerging Entrepreneurs: a non-profit platform that equips under-represented founders and future innovators with the tools to achieve their entrepreneurial, career, and educational ambitions.
CONNECTING THE DOTS:
"We develop strategies that broaden access to educational and entrepreneurial opportunities and produce positive economic and social outcomes."
ADVANCING CAPACITY:
"We help entrepreneurs design systems of collaboration, creating clearer pathways for startups, agencies, and institutions to align around shared goals and interests."
CHAMPIONING EQUITY:
"Lastly, we work to empower under-represented communities of founders & innovators through the equitable dissemination of social, intellectual, and capital investment."
serving communities since 2011:

COLLEGE READINESS PROGRAMS FOR VETERANS:
Attention all transitioning veterans with academic ambitions:
Columbia University’s “University’s Studies for Transitioning Veterans”
is designed to help veterans transition smoothly from military service to college, and helps them maximize their success once they arrive. This course will orient you to the norms and expectations of the college classroom, and offers strategies to ease your transition to help you achieve your academic goals; allowing you to make the most of your college education!
