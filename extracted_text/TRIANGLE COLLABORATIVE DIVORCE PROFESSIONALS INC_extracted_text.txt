What we’re about
We're a group of friendly, compassionate divorce professionals hosting a monthly online workshop on how to manage the emotional, financial, and legal aspects of separation and divorce. You can join us in person or on one of our live streams. We also welcome divorce professionals to participate in our training and networking events.

Sat, May 25, 2024, 1:00 PM UTC
divorcingRIGHT: Your Divorce Questions Answered
Link visible for attendees
Are you thinking about separation or divorce? Not sure where to start? Join us for a
free, online, interactive workshop
with legal, financial, and mental health professionals experienced in guiding you through all aspects of divorce in North Carolina.
During the workshop, you will:
Learn how to manage the strong emotions you may be experiencing as the result of your decision to separate.
Discuss how to design a parenting plan that protects your children from the negative impact of divorce.
Identify the information you need to gather and organize to be able to make sound financial decisions.
Understand the crucial differences between negotiating through attorneys, mediation, the collaborative law process, and litigation.
Explore how a supportive team can make the process go more smoothly.
Our presenters will answer all of your questions. Get the information you need to make informed choices for yourself, your family, and your future.
