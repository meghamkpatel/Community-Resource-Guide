Our Founder, Jevonda Perkins, is an outside of the box thinker and her passion for education shows. The Catch-up Center, Inc. focuses on providing support to students using Orton-Gillingham methods for reading, combined with unique Student Strength Connections. Having personal experience with experiencing the need for children to have affordable, multi-sensory tutoring the program was birthed! The unique approach created by Jevonda focuses on SSCs and building student relationships with their tutor as researched based tactics are used to increase student achievement.
