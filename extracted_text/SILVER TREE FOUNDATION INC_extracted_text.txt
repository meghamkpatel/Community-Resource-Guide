The Silver Tree Foundation empowers youth who are underserved by equipping them with job-related skills and expanding their horizons through new training opportunities. It is our desire to spark a revival that allows each life touched to not only dream, but attain a brilliant future — in their lives, community and the world.
The Silver Tree
Foundation
The Silver Tree Foundation awards grants for projects and programs in alignment with our mission and values – across the nation and around the globe. We review grant applications twice/year with awards ranging from $10,000-$100,000.
