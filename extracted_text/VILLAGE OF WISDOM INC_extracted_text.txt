Who We Are
We support family organizing and advocacy entities working to eliminate racial injustice in schools. To this
end
we develop tools and resources that help parents, teachers, and students create ideal learning environments for Black and Brown learners.
Mission
Close the academic opportunity gap by protecting the intellectual curiosity and positive racial self-concept of Black children through the love and wisdom of their families and communities
