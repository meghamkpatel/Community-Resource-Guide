Our Company Ethos
Our Story
Valiant opened its doors with 3 employees in 1961. We were founded based on the principles of unsurpassed integrity and impeccable business ethics, committed to working with our customers as partners and friends. Today our company has expanded in size and capabilities with scores of talented and experienced associates filling our ranks. However, after over 60 years our founding core business principles still remain woven into the very fabric of our organization, and we believe this sets us apart. We are proud to have long, established relationships with our clients, maintain a culture of customer service, and believe in adding value to every transaction and service.
