Beautified Saints Church
This is the official page of Beautified Saints Church, a global ministry with presence in the USA and Nigeria. To reach out to multitudes of people all over the world with the prophetic Word of grace.
Our Beautification Mandate was received in 2015 by our Prime Minister ✝️ Tochukwu Kemakolam and alongside  his wife ✝️Vanessa T. Kemakolam provides apostolic and prophetic directions to the global ministry.
This Beautification Mandate is —“To raise a tribe of beautified saints through the prophetic Word of Grace.”
Today, we see destinies, businesses, institutions and relationships being beautified through Word of Grace that we preach, teach and publish via the channels of Word ministrations, books, magazines, periodicals and digital platforms. The Word of Grace is beautifying destinies.
