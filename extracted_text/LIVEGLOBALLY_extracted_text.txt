Where in the world is LiveGlobally?
Durham, North Carolina
LiveGlobally has provided mental and physical support to thousands of vulnerable citizens in the Triangle in North Carolina with MindBody Centering Yoga trauma-informed mindfulness programs, such as The Boys & Girls Club and NC Women’s Correctional Facility.
Learn more…
﻿
Drake Bay, Costa Rica
LiveGlobally has implemented and expanded Water for Living, which provides hundreds of households with clean water access. Drake Bay is a place where many suffer from critical illnesses due to blackwater contamination.
Learn more…
Nairobi, Kenya
LiveGlobally has helped build the Ingrid School in the Montopeni community of Nairobi and sustains a living salary for all teachers, clean water and meals for all students and their families.
Learn more…
