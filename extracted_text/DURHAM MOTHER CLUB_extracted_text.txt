ABOUT US
JOIN NOW!
The Durham Mothers Club is a support network just for moms in Durham, North Carolina.
The club was founded in 1988 with the motto of Friendship - Support - Education. We are proud to provide all three and more to our members and we hope you will benefit from our motto too!
Whether you are a new mother, new to the Triangle, or just looking for some fun activities, the DMC has plenty to offer you.
The DMC currently has about 350 fellow moms as members in and around Durham. Our members range from full time at home moms to full time career moms and everything in between.
Join us to take advantage of our many members-only activities and resources, such as our online discussion forum.
There is a 30-day free trial membership and then membership is just $13 per year.
