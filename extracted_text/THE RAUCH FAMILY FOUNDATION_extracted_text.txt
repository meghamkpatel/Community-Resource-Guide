We support programs that are transformational,
evidence-based, and cost-effective.
Established in 1989 by Dudley and Ceci Rauch, the Rauch Family Foundation has invested across a variety of charitable
focus areas
with the largest single gift to date going to
Duke University Medical School
to establish a needs-based scholarship program for Medical Students, made in honor of the foundation’s late founders.
Based in Southern California, the Rauch Family Foundation focuses its US domestic grantmaking efforts on California’s Inland Empire.
