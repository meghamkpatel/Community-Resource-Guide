Tickled Pink Breast Cancer Support Inc. is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that provides complimentary healthy cuisine to single adult breast cancer survivors undergoing chemotherapy and radiation treatments.
We assist survivors currently in treatment who live in Raleigh, Durham and Chapel Hill and close surrounding cities in North Carolina.  We are advocates who support health and wellness during the fight of breast cancer through healthy eating. We help breast cancer survivors in treatment eat well and "smile through trials."
