RedApple Learning Campus
The RedApple Learning Campus (RALC) is a 501(c)(3) non-profit educational organization established in 2009.
RALC provides safe and high-quality Chinese after-school and summer camp programs for students of grades K-5 in Chapel Hill and Durham, NC. The emphasis of RALC is on establishing an atmosphere of love, support, and security, and on building self-confidence.
We offer classes in Chinese, mathematics, dance, music, painting, and crafts. We also offer full-day camps during Spring break and teacher workdays.
