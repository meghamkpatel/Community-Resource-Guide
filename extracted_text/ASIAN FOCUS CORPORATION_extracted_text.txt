Misson and Values
​
Asian Focus is a non-profit (501c3) organization formed to promote, sponsor and support programs to help Asian Americans and immigrants of all generations.​
​
​
We strive to help individuals and their families address the daily cultural and linguistic challenges to better acclimate into American society.
​
We foster synergy and encourage participation from agencies and skilled professionals; promoting education, diversity and cultural exchange services for all underserved groups towards the betterment of tomorrow.
