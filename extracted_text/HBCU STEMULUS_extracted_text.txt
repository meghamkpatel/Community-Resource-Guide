At The HBCU STEMulUS, we aim to make a lasting impact in the lives of all students at HBCU's regardless of race, color, and creed. Our initiatives and activities are designed to make students aware of the limitless opportunities in STEM and ways to access to these opportunities. But above all, passion for student success is the driving force for all we do."
Eisha S. Clyde, MS
