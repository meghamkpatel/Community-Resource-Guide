Our story
Dhingra family Foundation, USA is a non-profit charitable trust. It provides financial assistance to non-profit organizations in USA and non government organizations (NGOs) abroad in the field of education, health, sanitation & hygiene, Hindi language and literature. It is registered in the state of North Carolina and was founded in Morrisville, North Carolina, on January 1, 2014.
Dhingra family has been supporting education of children through Pratham, USA and Ekal Vidaylaya Foundation, USA for over last 20 years. Working with Pratham, starting in 2014 through 2017, Dhingra Foundation supported the education and completion of secondary school of 100 high school dropouts girls per year from underprivileged families in the State of Rajasthan, India.
