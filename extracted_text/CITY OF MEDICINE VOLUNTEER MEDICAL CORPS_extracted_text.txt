Who We Are:
We are a 501(c)3 nonprofit and member of the National Association of Free Clinics.
We recruit, train, and
manage
volunteers to help provide healthcare
resources
to schools and community groups serving children in Durham.
We were founded in the fall of 2020 to provide assistance with COVID-19 specific needs like temperature taking and arrival symptom screening.
In 2021 and beyond, our volunteers work with other local non-profits to bring vision screening and free glasses, free sports
physicals, and other high-quality preventive health care services to children and adolescents in Durham
County
.
