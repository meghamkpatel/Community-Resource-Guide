Durham Flight Homeschool Athletics
Durham Flight
is Durham's  NCHEAC (North Carolinians for Home Education Athletic Commission) approved competitive homeschool sports program, offering Basketball and Volleyball for local homeschooled athletes. Through NCHEAC, our teams are eligible to compete for Regional and State Tournament titles.
Durham Flight
is centrally located in Durham, NC.
Durham Flight Homeschool Athletics
uses athletics as a tool to help young men and women pursue godliness and excellence in every aspect of their lives.
