Welcome to the ALL-NETS Basketball Association
Exciting games await for 5' 10" and below basketball players! With the rim lowered to 9 feet.
All-Nets offers a FAST-PACED, HIGH-FLYING, EXCITEMENT-FILLED GAME THAT WILL FANS LOVE.
The ANBA's mission is to create space for shorter players to shoot their collective shot in the game of basketball that traditionally has been dominated by taller individuals. The ANBA has a league featuring 9-foot basketball goals to facilitate fast-paced, high-flying hoops for players 5' 9" or shorter.
