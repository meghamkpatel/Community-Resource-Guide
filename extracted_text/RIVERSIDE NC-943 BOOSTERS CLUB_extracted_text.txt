$30 for 30 years of RHS JROTC Campaign (30 for 30)
Sam Wolff is organizing this fundraiser to benefit Riverside NC-943 Boosters Club.
Learn more
Tax deductible
My name is Sam Wolff and on behalf of the Riverside Air Force Jr. ROTC (AFJROTC) booster club I’m raising money to
support our cadets
by ensuring we can maintain the learning and service opportunities that have been fundamental to the program at Riverside High School in Durham, North Carolina.
Please join us in celebrating 30 years of excellence and dedication
at Riverside High School’s NC-943 AFJROTC program! For three incredible decades, our cadets have epitomized honor, leadership, and service. They’ve soared to remarkable heights, instilling values that last a lifetime. We invite you to be a part of this extraordinary legacy by supporting our 30 for $30 fundraiser.
Your generous $30 donation honors the past and propels future leaders
on their journey to success. Help us continue to mold young minds, foster character, and promote citizenship. Together, we can ensure the next 30 years are even more remarkable than the last! Donate today and be part of our ongoing legacy of excellence.
This year,
we are raising $2,000
to support our 100 cadets.
Every dollar raised goes directly back to the cadets
to support activities such as adopt-a-family during the holidays and the end of year trip.
With your help we can ensure these events remain successful
as well as expand our core programs and explore additional events.
In general the JROTC program is a federal program, sponsored by the U.S. Armed Forces, and is offered as a course elective for each grade level in high school.
The overall focus of JROTC programs is inspiring better citizenship through leadership, character, and educational development.
Riverside's AFJROTC program is a cadet-run JROTC program that has been active since 1994.
The program emphasizes the values of citizenship, civic duty, leadership, personal responsibility and self-worth.
Upon completing three years of the program, students are afforded several opportunities that include competition for college scholarships, direct application to military academics, and the ability to enlist in the military at a higher pay grade than typical enlistees.
The program's curriculum emphasizes aerospace science, leadership education, physical fitness, and drill. The cadets actively participate in community events and activities.
Riverside’s AFJROTC Mission Statement and Goals
The mission of the Riverside AFJROTC program is to develop citizens of character dedicated to serving their nation and community. The goals of the program are to instill values of citizenship, give service to the United States, develop a personal responsibility, and instill a sense of accomplishment in high school students.
