Welcome
The Institute
The Pastoral Care and Counseling Institute is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing spiritual support in many forms to the wider community. This includes counseling, psychotherapy, clinical training, and consultation and education in a variety of settings and under various initiatives. We have carried out this mission for over thirty-five years. The services we offer are sensitive to the diversity of individual, family, social and spiritual needs and seek to address cultural and systemic dimensions. At the center of our mission is the treatment of the whole person in service of the whole human and more than human family.
Our staff has advanced education, training and North Carolina State Board certification in the fields of pastoral psychotherapy, counseling, marriage and family therapy and clinical supervision.
