What does the PTO do?
Establish and maintain a working relationship among parents, school and community.
Support school improvement teams and promote the finest education possible for the students at Mangum Elementary.
Expand technology and supplement equipment and supplies.
Increase student safety and security.
Develop programs and projects that will support or enrich the curriculum.
Enhance the quality of education by raising funds for school supplies or programs that fall outside the school budget.
Dear Mangum Families,
On behalf of the Mangum Elementary Parent Teacher Organization (PTO), it is our pleasure to welcome you and your family to Mangum. We look forward to getting to know you and your child(ren).
As PTO Board members, our responsibilities include informing you about the PTO, inviting you to be an active member and more importantly, encouraging you to get involved in our school community.
We are a group of dedicated parents (like you) who have volunteered our time for the enhancement of our children’s Mangum experience. Our primary objective as a PTO is to provide our school with funds, programs, resources and services that will enrich and maximize the education of everyone in the learning community.  For more information regarding our PTO and many programs, please visit the PTO PROGRAMS page on our website.
We are available throughout the school year to help make your transition to Mangum as seamless as possible. In the meantime, please feel free to contact us with any questions or just to introduce yourself and say hello.
Wishing you a great school year!
The Mangum PTO Board
