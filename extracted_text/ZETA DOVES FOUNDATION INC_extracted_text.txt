Our Mission
Zeta D.O.V.E. Foundation is a 501 (c)(3) nonprofit organization. The Foundation’s principle activities and purposes are three-fold; to promote the total development of children and youth who aspire to excel through seminars that enhance the mind, body and spirit; to award scholarships for higher education; and to conduct educational programs for expectant mothers to insure the health of their unborn babies.
