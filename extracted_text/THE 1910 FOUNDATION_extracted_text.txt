WHO WE ARE
As the official Sigma Epsilon Chapter foundation of Tau Epsilon Phi Fraternity, we provide educational scholarships to the chapter's undergraduates as well as educational programing. As a 501C(3), EIN: 87-3554905, donations are tax deductible (check with your tax professional).
WAYS WE HELP
The Focus of Our Efforts
Scholarship Awards
1910 provides the opportunity for undergraduate chapter members to qualify for merit-based scholarships.
Allowing Alumni To Give Back
1910 is the vehicle that allows the alumni from the chapter to help the undergraduates financially in the spirit of being Bridgebuilders.
