99th Most Worshipful Prince Hall Grand Lodge Annual Communication Notice
The 99th Annual Communication of the Most Worshipful Prince Hall Grand Lodge will be held at the Four Points by Sheraton, 5311 South Howell Avenue Milwaukee, Wisconsin 53207 on Sunday, Monday, and Tuesday, June 9th-11th, 2024.
Registration will be $165.00 + $5.29 credit card processing fee when paying online total cost $170.29, deadline is May 25th, 2024, which includes a banquet ticket with your registration.
The cost for an extra banquet ticket is $50.00 + $2.00 credit card processing fee when paying online total cost $52.00.
On-site registration will be $225.00, and it may not include a banquet ticket.
