encouraged by grace
Knowing Jesus changes everything. When we surrender to Him, our sins are forgiven, our future is secure. We are free.
But living a life of godliness—aligned with God’s Word—is challenging. Especially with the enemy of our souls hissing in our ears. And although he cannot take our salvation, he can steal our joy and cause us to stumble.
My prayer is that these posts encourage and inspire you. I hope the stories are relatable and the eternal Truths reassuring…and that you are encouraged by grace along the journey Home.
Julianne Winkler Smith
