Corporate Responsibility
Rise Up, Reach Out provides the means for all Pinnacle employees to give back to our communities. Through generous company and employee donations, Pinnacle provides grants to eligible 501c3 charities through our partner The Community Foundation of Greater Huntsville. Employees also participate in various volunteer opportunities throughout the year.
Grant Information
In support of 501c3 organizations, Rise Up, Reach Out provides financial grants. Our grant application process begins with an application. To complete an application, submit your information in the form below, and a member of Rise Up, Reach Out Board of Advisors will contact you in a timely manner.
