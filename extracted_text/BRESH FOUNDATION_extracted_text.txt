Founded in Cleveland in 1928, the Brush Foundation has a proud history of funding essential and innovative work in the field of reproductive health and rights. Currently, the Brush Foundation strives to create, foster, and drive change by supporting people and organizations with aligned visions of reproductive health, rights, and justice in Ohio.
