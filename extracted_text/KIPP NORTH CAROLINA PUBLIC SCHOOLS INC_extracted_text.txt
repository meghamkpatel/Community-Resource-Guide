At KIPP North Carolina, we are college & career bound.
At KIPP North Carolina, our students are given the tools and support to learn, grow, and achieve. Our teachers and staff believe wholeheartedly that ALL children can go to college, succeed in any career, and conquer any goal they decide to pursue.
Enroll with us. Join us. Partner with us.
About Us
KIPP North Carolina is a network of free, non-profit, high-performing, college-preparatory public charter schools providing an academically rigorous and joyous school experience to 3,100 students in grades K-12 in Charlotte, Durham, Halifax, and Gaston. Additionally, KIPP NC supports several thousand alumni on their journey to and through high school, college, and career. We are the only public school district in the state serving an equal number of rural and urban students.
