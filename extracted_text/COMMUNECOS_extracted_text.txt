Welcome to
CommunEcos!
We are a small environmental education and advocacy non-profit based in Durham, NC, USA.  We work with others to build a just, climate/energy transition to preserve our only planet for future generations.  Over the years we have sponsored close to 500 educational events. Check out our projects and organizing pages to learn about our work and our events page for more on what's coming up.
