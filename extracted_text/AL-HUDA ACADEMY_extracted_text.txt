Principal Nayeela being awarded Educator of the Year award on April 2024 by Hanover Park’s Mayor Craig.
This recognition is a testament to Principal Nayeela's unwavering dedication, hard work, and passion for education. Her commitment to providing our students with an exceptional learning experience has not only enriched their lives but has also made a significant impact on our school community.

About Us
Al-Huda Academy is a full time Islamic school providing religious and academic education for preschool-7th grade, Monday to Friday. The school was established in 2007 by dedicated community members.

Our student culture plays a key role in the experience at
Al-Huda Academy. We’ve created a safe and accessible environment where students are encouraged to discuss ideas, collaborate on projects, share their work and receive feedback with pride. We give students the foundation to reach their potential and gain confidence both academically and socially.
We need your support!
Al-Huda Academy was established so students from all backgrounds would have a formal education that could transform their lives and positively contribute to society. Since then, we have held true to that vision because of the generosity of countless community members and businesses who share similar values. Please consider donating.
