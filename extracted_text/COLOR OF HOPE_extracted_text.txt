About Color of Hope
Color of Hope (C.O.H) is a 501(c)(3) U.S. based organization working to improve the quality of life of Haitians in the U.S. and Haiti. We provide access to education, platforms for constructive dialogue, resources and a technological environment for youth in Haiti to develop 21st century skills. Color of Hope is designed to engage families and communities in Haiti and abroad to come together, support each other and build together.
