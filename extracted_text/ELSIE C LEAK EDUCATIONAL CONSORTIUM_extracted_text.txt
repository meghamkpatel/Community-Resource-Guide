2016 Emerald Award Honorees:  Biographies
Raquel Battle,
(Facet:  International Trends and Services)
Rachel is the President and Founder of the Blissfulsage Foundation, which is committed to research and the raising of awareness of cancer as well as providing hospice care for those living with cancer and diabetes, internationally.
Dr. Elsie Leak, (Facet:  Services to Youth)
Dr. Leak is the Founder and Current Dire
ctor of the Elsie C. Leak Educational Consortium that partners with educational entities in the community to improve student academic achievements using highly trained educators.
Nancy Pinckney, (Facet:  The Arts)
Nancy has been the Director of the NCCU Dance Group for over 45 years. She embraces an open environment that fosters dance artists with members of the NCCU Dance Group being key members of local performing companies.
Dr. Portia W. Rochelle, (Facet:  National Trends and Services)
Dr. Rochelle is the founder of an area ministry that sponsors a women’s clothing closet. The ministry provides wardrobe assistance, resume preparation, mentoring and life encouragement, among other things.
Dr. Kamala Latori Uzzell-Jones, (Facet:  Health and Human Services)
Dr. Uzzell-Jones is a nationally recognized licensed professional counselor and supervisor, as well as being a national board certified counselor, educator, and higher education administrator with over 15 years of experience.
