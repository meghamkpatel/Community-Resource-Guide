Health Partnership Groundbreaking Celebration
Thank you for joining us virtually for the groundbreaking of Spokane’s new center for medical education, health sciences, and innovation being developed by McKinstry along with co-developer Gonzaga University. The event, hosted by the University of Washington School of Medicine, Gonzaga University, and McKinstry will celebrated this unique public-private-private partnership.
﻿
UW-GU Health Partnership Breaks New Ground
Construction Begins on Medical, Health Education and Innovation Center.
A shared commitment for the greater good
The Health Partnership is expanding and enhancing top-ranked medical education in Spokane to improve the health and vitality of our region.
