Our Work
Denver Scholarship Foundation (DSF)
is a nonprofit making college possible and building the educational and economic future of Denver by collaborating with
Denver Public Schools (DPS)
high school students, families, and partners statewide to increase access and completion of post-high school learning and expand generational exposure to career and entrepreneurial opportunities.

Why our approach works
The DSF nationally recognized, evidence-based three-part program consists of support systems, actionable tools, and scholarships to help guide Denver students to lifelong success.
Learn more about our approach and impact

College Access
Our full-time College Advisors work proactively with DPS 9th-12th graders at our DSF Future Centers located inside DPS high schools.
Read more
Scholarships
We award need-based scholarships to DPS graduates attending a community college, university, or technical college in Colorado.

Success
We partner directly with colleges in Colorado to provide DSF Scholars with additional financial aid and ongoing support to help them succeed through college, career and beyond.
