Our History
Our History
Dream Keepers International partners & supports organizations that align with our mission to help children to reach their dreams. Seeing a need for dedicated nonprofit work in this area, we formed our organization to provide sensible solutions & strategies to help better the lives of Jamaican children.

Our Mission
Creating opportunities for children ages 6-18 to get the best start on achieving their dreams. Our partners & volunteers provide the momentum that helps us affect change in a positive way. Combining emotional intelligence, hands-on experience, & our data-driven models, we provide solutions that make a long-lasting impact
