Durham Regional theatre is AN INTENTIONALLY MULTI-RACIAL, MULTI-ETHNIC & INTERGENERATIONAL THEATRE, CREATING & PRESENTING WORKS THAT TENACIOUSLY REFLECT THE RICH human DIVERSITY in DURHAM, NC
DRT believes that theater is a powerful vehicle for intellectual, emotional and ethical human growth, and that by telling good stories together we can realize our highest visions.   DRT intends to provide a context for diverse communities to purposefully bridge cultural barriers through creative collaboration.
