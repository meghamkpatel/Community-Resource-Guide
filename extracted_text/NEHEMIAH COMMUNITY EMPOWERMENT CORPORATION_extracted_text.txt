OUR MISSION IS
TO SERVE THE COMMUNITY
Nehemiah Empowerment Community Center is a non-profit organization founded in 2008 focused on empowering and enriching the lives of at-risk youth, teens and adults in the communities we serve. Our focus is on STEM, robotic and I.T. career exploration.  We are creating career awareness and diversity in these lucrative fields.  Through our impact, we are changing lives.
The Nehemiah Center knows that our strength lies not only in the words we stand by, but most importantly through the actions of our initiatives.
​
Our STEM motto is:

& BEYOND!

Enrich
