About Us
The executive director and founder of All My Children Child Care Center, Paulette Muhammad has devoted over 40 years  supporting the early learning and development of children 0-12 years old.  Mrs. Muhammad is not your ordinary child care provider; she has opened her home to care for children and families, since opening her first family child care in New York. Ms. Muhammad has dedicated her entire life to enrich the development of young children and support to families in need  She remains abreast of best practices in early care and education and advocates for all young children served in the early care and education system.
curriculum
All My Children Child Care Center provides a child and family centered learning environment. We observe and respond to the unique needs of each child development and learning.
enrichments
We use “Play” as an opportunity for children to learn create use their imagination, exploration, and physical, cognitive, language and social-emotional development.
We value the cultural and linguistic language of each child and their family.
community
All My Children focuses on helping family in the following ways free meal service for families, parent night out, and family clothing closet.
We honor the family and childcare partnership by working  together to support each child and their familiy’s life-long success.
