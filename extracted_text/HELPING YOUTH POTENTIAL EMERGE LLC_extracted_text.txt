Help for Job Seekers
A full continuum of personalized service
EMERGE has deep roots in North Minneapolis and Cedar-Riverside neighborhoods. For over 15 years, we’ve been helping people find good jobs and start careers. We can help, if you are facing obstacles to employment. Many of our job coaches have ‘been there, done that.’ They have overcome similar challenges to yours. If you are ready to earn higher wages and jump-start a career, contact EMERGE today. We are ready to be your partner, to help you meet YOUR needs.
