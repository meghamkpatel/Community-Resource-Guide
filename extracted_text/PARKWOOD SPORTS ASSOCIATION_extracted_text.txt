Welcome to Parkwood Rec!
PARA offers baseball, softball, basketball, cheerleading, wrestling, and flag football to boys and girls ages 3-18.  Our goal is to teach and develop our players while instilling life-lessons and values such as character, team work, discipline, respect and sportsmanship.
PARA's dedicated coaches and volunteers strive to provide an enriching experience for every child in a fun and safe atmosphere.
