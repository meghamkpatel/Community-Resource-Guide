Show Your Pride.
Ohioans: Show your pride in Ohio’s aviation heritage and aerospace industry with a “Leaders in Flight” license plate that supports NAHA and Wright “B” Flyer Inc, and all-volunteer group dedicated to flying and educating the world with a look-a-like of the first production airplane.
NAHA Leaders in Flight license plates are available for your passenger vehicles, non-commercial trucks, recreational vehicles, house vehicles, and non-commercial trailers. You can also order personalized plates (up to six characters).
The Leaders in Flight license plate program supports NAHA’s efforts to preserve and promote aviation heritage. The state contributes $15 of every registration to Wright “B” Flyer; half of that amount goes to the National Aviation Heritage Area.
Order plates online directly from the BMV.
Here’s how:
Go to oplates.com and click on “Ohio Plates.”
Follow the online instructions and when the screen appears, select “add a logo” to your plate.
Scroll down the list to organizations and select “Leaders in Flight.”
Continue the steps to order your plates.
The National Aviation Heritage Alliance (NAHA) is a private, not-for-profit corporation designated by Congress as the management entity of the National Aviation Heritage Area.
