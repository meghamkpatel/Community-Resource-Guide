established in 1998, NBN Sports started as a small family owned custom basketball uniform business in San Jose, California. as the name grew, so did the uniform offerings and NBN started making uniforms for a variety of sports. but the fans wanted more, so we gave it to them, and started creating something we like to call “fan-wear” – warm-ups, custom t-shirts, sweatshirts, bags, and more all to match the uniforms, and keep the spirit of the team alive.
now everyone shows up in style!
this led the way to the expansion of custom clothing, promotional  apparel, promotional items  and swag of all kinds. -we silkscreen, embroider,  digitally print, emboss/deboss, you name it! we are proud to be of service of any  organization - sports club, school club, corporate, church, and any family/friends reunion!
