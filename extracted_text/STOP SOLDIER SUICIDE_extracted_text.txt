Military suicide
has claimed more lives
than we’ve lost in most major conflicts,
including World War I, the Vietnam War, and the Global War on Terror.
We need to work hard and fast, together, to solve this problem once and for all.
Join us in the fight to END veteran and service member suicide.
We Cannot Stand By.
Stop Soldier Suicide is the only national nonprofit focused solely on solving the issue of suicide among U.S. veterans and service members. We have an aggressive goal to reduce the military suicide rate by 40% no later than 2030.
Our R&D team uses advanced data science to gain new insights into the root causes of suicidal thoughts and behavior.

We are a veteran-founded and veteran-led organization that understands the needs of those who reach out to us.

Our suicide-specific clinical programming was designed specifically to help the military community.

Struggling with Thoughts of Suicide?
Our ROGER wellness service provides counseling, crisis intervention, and suicide prevention–100% free for U.S. veterans and service members.
