We are Changing a Generation. For 20 years Changing a Generation’s mission has been to provide an affordable safe learning environment for families and children to learn, and a chance to reach their fullest potential. To empower families with the tools to address the challenges designed to destroy the family. Changing a Generation provides a private Christian educational daycare for 0-5, an Academy from first grade to twelfth grade. For more details visit
www.changingageneration.org
