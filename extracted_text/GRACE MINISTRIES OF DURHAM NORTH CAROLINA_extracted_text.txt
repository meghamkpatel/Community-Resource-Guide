Welcome To Grace Church of Durham!
We hope you’ll find this to be a place where you can get to know a little bit more about us and what we’re doing in the Durham, NC area. Feel free to take some time to learn about our history, our vision and our staff. While you’re here, be sure to  check out what our ministries are doing, watch or listen to a weekend  message and find out how you can get connected!
We’d love for you to join us for
Sunday Morning Worship at 10:30AM
and for
Wednesday Family Night at 6:00PM
.  It’s our prayer that your life will be changed as you come in contact  with the good news of Jesus! If we can serve you in any way, please  check out our “I’m New” page or contact us and we will be in touch with you very soon!
Be blessed,
Pastor K.T. Lowery
