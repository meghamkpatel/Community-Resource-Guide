Love God ~ Grow Together ~ Serve the World
Welcome to Grace Lutheran Church & School - a congregation made up of people of all ages from all walks of life, brought together through our shared commitment to and love for our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ. We worship an amazing God and we desire to glorify His name and share His love in everything we do.
Grace Lutheran Church began 1961, as a small congregation in the heart of La Plata. In 1967, we moved to our current location and continue to remain an active and engaged member of our community. If you are seeking a church home, we invite you to ours.
Grace Lutheran Preschool began in 1974. Today, Grace Lutheran School offers a comprehensive accredited academic program for our more than 250 students, age 2 through 8th grade.
