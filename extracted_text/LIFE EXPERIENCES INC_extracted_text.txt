Life Experiences at a glance
Life Experiences is a nonprofit organization based in Cary, NC offering financial assistance, guidance, and advocacy to adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities (I/DD) and their families.
Learn more about our mission, our vision, and how we go about making the changes we want to see.
Our Vision
For every adult with I/DD, equal access to programming and employment.
