UNC Eshelman School of Pharmacy Foundation
The
UNC Eshelman School of Pharmacy Foundation
was established in December 1946 to support the UNC Eshelman School of Pharmacy. The Foundation secures financial resources for the betterment of the School in its quest to transform the teaching and learning of pharmacy.
Mission
The Foundation fosters and promotes the profession of pharmacy and pharmaceutical education and research exclusively at the UNC Eshelman School of Pharmacy by securing and stewarding financial resources to support the mission of the School.
Objectives
Provide support to faculty and staff of the UNC Eshelman School of Pharmacy.
Extend scholarships to deserving students.
Promote support for the graduate program.
Deliver needed capital improvements.
Identify and attract investment in public-private partnerships that accelerate change in pharmacy and pharmaceutical education, research and healthcare worldwide.
