Seaforth's Hawks Nation Athletic Membership Club
Annual Monogram Club member contributions (formerly known as the Founding Member Club) support Seaforth High School Athletics. By contributing to The Monogram Club, members make a lasting impact on the current, former and future generations of Seaforth Athletics.
