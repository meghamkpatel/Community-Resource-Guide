Do you love tie-dye and dancing bears? Live music and counterculture? The Portland Pickles take on the Nanaimo Night Owls while we search for peace, love, and unity in a world of alternatives to mainstream society. Keep on Truckin’. Celebrate the Grateful Dill at Walker Stadium and get your tickets now!
Presented by Happy Mountain Kombucha
Rockin’ The Walk :
Enjoy a free, live pregame performance from a local PDX band @ 6:00 PM. Bands will be announced at a later date!
Merch Special:
Our fifth artist series! Each season, the Pickles partner with 10 local artists and create a limited edition collection, featuring 50 tee shirts, 25 posters, and 50 beer koozies- available for pre-purchase with your tickets. Limited quantity available in stadium day of game
Activities:
Grateful Dill mud wrestling. Other weird stuff.
