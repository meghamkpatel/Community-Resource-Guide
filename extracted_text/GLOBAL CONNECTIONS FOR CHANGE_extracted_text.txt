Global Connections for Change is a 501 (c)(3) non-profit organization that creatively connects people and communities across the world to make a sustainable impact and create positive change.
Our Vision →
Moshi Youth CAB
The Moshi Youth Community Advisory Board supports a diverse group of young people who share a desire learn more about health issues impacting young people in Kilimanjaro region of Tanzania. Moshi Youth CAB teaches leadership skills and builds community with other groups in the region.
Read More →
Douglas Thomas Memorial CARE Fund
The CARE Fund provides short term emergency financial assistance to HIV+ people. While medications have improved the lives of many patients, there are those who remain in tremendous need. There are few programs designed to help them in a time of crisis.
Read More →
DART CAB
The DART CAB sponsors outreach educational events for at-risk populations. These events are centered around HIV-awareness building, demystifying medical clinical research, and other educational topics that people infected or affected by HIV/AIDS experience.
Read More →
Alcaldía Maya
The Mayan Council works within a small municipality located in the department of El Quiché, Guatemala to empower the municipality's indigenous population.
