Charitable Giving at Carolina Meadows
The Power of Community
Carolina Meadows knows what it means to be part of a community. We have a proud history of caring which radiates inward and outward to support employees and residents and to create a vibrant community life.
Our Mission
Your gift to the Carolina Meadows Foundation makes a difference, enhancing programs and services to increase the quality of life within our community. Charitable giving helps Carolina Meadows aspire to its vision to be the best place to live and work.
The Carolina Meadows Foundation, Inc. was established in 2018 for the purpose of supporting Carolina Meadows, Inc. It creates a structured vehicle for the generosity that already exists, and it connects our generous gift-givers with the areas of greatest need within the Caroline Meadows community.  The Foundation allows a broader pathway for generous donors to contribute to the current and future success of Carolina Meadows, Inc.
The Impact of Your Gift
Your gifts – big and small – make an impact. You make the difference.
Carolina Meadows is a place where people care deeply for each other, and it is a place worth giving to. Your gift makes a difference, enhancing programs and services to increase the quality of life within Carolina Meadows. Our donors include residents, family, friends and employees. They are inspired to give for different reasons and share the desire to make a positive impact.

Our Family of Funds
The Foundation has four main Funds: Fund for the Future, Fund for Campus Life, Fund for Employees and Resident Association Gift and Remembrance.  Each offers unique giving aspects.  Whatever your passion or interest, the Carolina Meadows Foundation can connect you with a meaningful and unique giving opportunity.
