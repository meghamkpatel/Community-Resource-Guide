Chocolate Soul Revival
A Healing Justice Initiative for Black Women & Girls. We are committed to the transformative work of healing, justice and liberation that centers the lives and voices of those most deeply impacted by systemic oppression.
Home: Welcome
This Work is a Living Declaration that...
➢ Mental, emotional, and spiritual wellness, informed by the cultural practices of our Ancestors, is our birthright.
​
➢ Culturally specific, trauma informed, politicized healing work led by those from the very communities it's designed for, is essential.
​
➢ Healing centered organizing increases our capacity to strategize and co-create freedom, justice, and equity from a place of wholeness and clarity.
