Reasons to Love Wildflower Cottage
Intimate Environment
A cozy, home-like learning environment where children can thrive, learn and play.
Reggio Emilia Approach
Children are naturally curious learners, capable of shaping their own learning process.
Intergenerational Experiences
Intergenerational experiences with senior adult volunteers in the community.
Natural
Learning
Natural materials—both indoors and out—provide children with a sense of calm and wonder.
Eco-Healthy
Child Care
By reducing toxins, we help prevent illnesses like asthma, certain learning disabilities, and even some cancers.
A Good Neighbor
Wildflower Cottage is mindful of the part we play in our community, both locally and in the world.
Conscious Discipline
A social and emotional intelligence classroom management program that empowers teachers and students.
School Readiness
Kindergarten is not an end-goal, but yet another beautiful step in your child’s journey.
