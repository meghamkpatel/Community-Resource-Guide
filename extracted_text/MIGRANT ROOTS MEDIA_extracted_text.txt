Central Americans have been fleeing the
economic, social, and political unrest caused by U.S-fueled civil wars and genocide
,
multinational corporate plundering
, and
corrupt governments
. Once they establish themselves in the U.S, remitting becomes a way to show love, care, commitment, sacrifice, and to reassure family members that they are not forgotten. Providing financial assistance is also viewed as a sign of “making it,” and, unfortunately,
many may even think that the long-term family separation and the psychological trauma is worth it
