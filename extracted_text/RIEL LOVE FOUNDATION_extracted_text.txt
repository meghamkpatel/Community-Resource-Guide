OUR PROMISE
In honor of Zuriel Kieran Overby Robinson, we commit to blessing the lives of others. Whether it be through an encouraging word, a hug, a scholarship, or through meeting basic practical needs--we want to spread love the "Riel Way". Zuriel's way was the Way of Jesus Christ. We wholeheartedly follow this example.

OUR MISSION
We want to honor our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ, by carrying the torch of Zuriel's legacy. Zuriel's heart was to glorify God in how he loved and how he served. He was a light to so many. Now we have the privilege of being the same.
