CEM at Work
Since 1982, Christian Eye Ministry has worked diligently to improve eye care in Africa and Central America. In addition to basic eye services and training of healthcare professionals, CEM also conducts research projects focused on common eye diseases in these populations. Our volunteer doctors were the first to perform cataract surgery with intraocular lenses, glaucoma surgery with antimetabolites, and glaucoma drainage implant surgery in Ghana.
