Congratulations, L4AA!
At the March 2024 national convention in Knoxville, TN of the National Society of Pershing Rifles Alumni Association, L-4AA was recognized as the NSPRAA
“Company of the Year”
for 2023. The citations reads as follows:
“For providing outstanding service to the Fraternity and to their membership with the best overall set of programs and activities, and recognizes outstanding work, participation, and accomplishments by the Alumni company.”
Also, during this year’s meeting, L4AA member and director, Thomas Swanson was honored. During the alumni dinner, Thomas became the first recipient from L-4AA to receive the
Unsung Hero Award
for his Chairmanship of the Scholarship Committee.
Company L-4 Reinstated During Pershing Rifles National Convention
Pershing Rifles Company L-4 at NC State University was reinstated during the Pershing Rifles National Convention at Myrtle Beach, SC, March 12 - 15, 2020.  Please read the article, accessible at the button below, for an account of the activities during the reinstatement.
PROCON to NATCON
The L-4 Alumni Association, Inc. is a company alumni association of the National Society of Pershing Rifles Alumni Association – a partner organization of The Pershing Rifles Group, Inc.  The purpose of the  L-4 Alumni Association, Inc. is to support the objectives of the National Society of Pershing Rifles Alumni Association and continue the bonds of brotherhood established by the National Society of Pershing Rifles in 1894.
