Justin McCarrell
October 15, 1987 — November 7, 2023
Taylors, SC
Mr. Justin Barrett McCarrell, 36, of Taylors, South Carolina, passed away Tuesday, November 7, 2023, at Prisma Health Greenville Memorial Hospital.   He was a son of Barrett Parks and Dosephine Amanda McCarrell Brown.
Surviving:  in addition to his parents, he is survived by one daughter, Rhythm Chamblee of Greenville, SC; one son, Amaru Langhorn of Lynchburg, VA; three sisters, Franchetta (Terrance) Savage of Simpsonville, SC, Kasey Parks of Greenville, SC and Asia Parks of Atlanta, GA; a host of nieces, nephews, other relatives and friends.
Funeral Services: Friday, November 17, 2023, 1:00pm,  at Watkins Garrett & Woods Mortuary, with interment in New Salem Baptist Church, Tigerville, SC.
