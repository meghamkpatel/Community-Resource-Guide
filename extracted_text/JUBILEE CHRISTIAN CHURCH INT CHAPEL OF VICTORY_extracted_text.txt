Welcome Home
Jubilee Christian Church International – Chapel of Victory is a place where believers love God with a passion and care for the community.
This is a place of true worship, fervent prayer and bible based teachings. Come and worship with us at one of our various weekly programs for a divine experience.
You don’t have to worry about fitting in; we are a mix of different nations and cultures from many parts of the world welcoming everyone with open arms into the presence of the Lord.
Are you weary and thirsty? Do you have a longing to encounter God in a unique way? Chapel of Victory is the place to be.
We look forward to seeing you soon!
Pastors Bisi & Toyin Tofade
