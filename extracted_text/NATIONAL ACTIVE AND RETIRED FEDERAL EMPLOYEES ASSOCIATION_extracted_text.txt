Watch Webinars On Demand
NARFE provides expert guidance on federal benefits issues directly affecting current and former federal employees, as well as tips and tools to help ensure a safe and secure retirement for the federal community. Webinars are free to NARFE members.

Advocacy
NARFE’s national legislative program is advanced in Congress by a team of registered lobbyists backed by an informed network of grassroots activists in every state and congressional district, as well as a member-supported Political Action Committee (PAC).

Federal Benefits Institute

On May 3, President Joe Biden issued a proclamation to honor the dedication of public servants, and Rep. Gerry Connolly, D-VA, introduced H.
