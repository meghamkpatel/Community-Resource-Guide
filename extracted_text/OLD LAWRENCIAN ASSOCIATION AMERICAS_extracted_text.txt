An Old Lawrencian for Life
Every student of the Lawrence School Lovedale who has completed an academic year or more is eligible for life membership to the Old Lawrencians Association and its global community.
Students who have passed out of Class 12 from the year 1990 automatically qualify and are registered for life membership. The fee for becoming a registered OLA member is deducted automatically from the ‘Caution Deposit’ at the time of graduation.
