Helping people live, work and play in Milwaukee.
By joining the Chamber, you are supporting our mission to invest capital, grow business and create jobs.
​Chamber member provides access to numerous programs and perks, but at its core, you are investing in:
Talent
Collaborating to develop, retain and attract the talent employers need to thrive in the Milwaukee region
Growth
Fueling growth by facilitating corporate expansion, attraction and entrepreneurship
Livability
Investing in assets that enhance the Milwaukee region
