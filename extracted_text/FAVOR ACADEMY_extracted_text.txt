About Favor Academy of Excellence
Our Heart Work
Mission: To provide quality educational programs and connect resources to students in under resourced communities.   We promote growth and sustainability in communities with economic disadvantages through increased access to educational opportunities.
Vision: We believe every student deserves an opportunity to thrive!  We believe access is the conduit to equity. We believe in building, supporting and sustaining education programs that are responsive, relevant and discrimination free.
Motto: Anything Perceivable, Is Achievable....So Think Higher!!!
