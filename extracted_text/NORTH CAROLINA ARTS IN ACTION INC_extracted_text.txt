INFORMATION FOR SCHOOLS
North Carolina Arts in Action is a 501 (c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to bringing in-school and after-school dance programs to elementary schools in Orange, Durham and Wake counties in North Carolina. Through fiscal partnerships with individual schools, along with private and public funds, we inspire children through the arts.
If you are interested in learning more about our programs and how we work with individual schools, please contact Artistic Director, Suzi Landis, at
suzi.ncartsinaction.org
or 919-619-0483.
