Aunties Against Abuse
Educate. Equip. Empower. Advocate.
Aunties Against Abuse (A3) is a Jesus led non-profit ministering to young ladies ages 5-18.
​
Our 2024 Theme is: "L.E.A.D.E.R.S.H.I.P.".

Educate.
to provide information and instruction relevant to being a young lady in the twenty-first century
Equip.
to prepare young ladies to be critical thinkers, honest communicators, goal-oriented individuals and productive
​members of society
Empower.
to inspire young ladies to be who God created them to be without apology; to build trust and ​safety in relationships
Advocate.
to partner with licensed professionals and established agencies and organizations to help with professional
and advocacy services in support of our young ladies emotional, mental, physical and spiritual health
