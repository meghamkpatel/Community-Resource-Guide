Meet J. E. Shepard Magnet Middle's new principal Claude Archer
Claude Archer
comes to DPS from Edgecombe County Public Schools' West Edgecombe Middle School, where in addition to serving as principal he participated on district committees on equity and alternative education. He was previously an assistant principal at Rocky Mount, Nash Central, and Southern Nash high schools. He also taught business education, coached athletics, and coordinated in-school suspension programs in high schools. Archer holds an MBA from Walden University and received his administrative licensure and secondary teacher certification from East Carolina University.
