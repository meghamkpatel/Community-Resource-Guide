WHO WE ARE...
Spread The Fire
is a non-profit ministry to Asia and The United States.  God birthed
Spread The Fire Ministries
in the hearts of Francis and Jean Pereira in 2015 while living in the USA after having spent many years ministering in Asia.  Francis and Jean's own stories are a testimony to the power of God's transforming love and grace.  As a result, they have a passion to share the love they've experienced through Jesus with the nations of the world.
