UNC Management Company
UNC Management Company, Inc. (“UNCMC”) is a nonprofit organization that provides investment management services to the University of North Carolina System, its constituent institutions and their affiliated endowments and foundations. UNCMC manages the long-term assets of its investors through the UNC Investment Fund, a commingled investment vehicle with assets of over $10.6 billion.
(1)
UNCMC also provides investment management and administrative services to other investment vehicles that support the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.
