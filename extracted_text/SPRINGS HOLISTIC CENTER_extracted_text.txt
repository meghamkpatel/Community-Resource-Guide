Springs is a safe haven for children and adolescents ages 5-19, and their immediate family members. We offer life changing therapeutic tools, to heal and change families; by treating the entire family as the client.
Mission
To create a safe haven that provides therapeutic services to children, adolescents, and their immediate family members; in a holistic environment dedicated to their personal growth and change.
​
Vision
Empowered children, balanced families, and healthy minds.
